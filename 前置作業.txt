因為單個檔案太大推不上去，所以每次打開codepace要先重新下載
curl -O https://dl.google.com/dl/cloudsdk/channels/rapid/downloads/google-cloud-sdk-423.0.0-linux-x86_64.tar.gz
tar -xf google-cloud-sdk-423.0.0-linux-x86_64.tar.gz
./google-cloud-sdk/install.sh --quiet
安裝好後跑
./google-cloud-sdk/bin/gcloud init


結束後要刪掉google-cloud-sdk-423.0.0-linux-x86_64.tar.gz
